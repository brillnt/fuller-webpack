Meet THE CONFIGURATOR

Where Design
Meets Data

At the heart of our homebuilding process is the Fuller Configurator—a powerful tool that centralizes all design data, serving as the single source of truth for every home plan.

Powered by BIM technology, it ensures precision, efficiency, and control over every step of the building process.

Real-Time Personalization

Instantly configure facades, layouts, finishes, and even structural features, all within the parameters of Fuller’s smart component and sub-assembly system.

Automatic Takeoffs

Accurate material lists are generated directly from the system, eliminating delays, cost discrepancies, and manual guesswork.

Permit-Ready Plans

Finalized, submission-ready plans are created automatically, saving you time and architectural costs for every home.

Supply chain

Direct Material Sourcing

By leveraging precise takeoff data from the Configurator, Fuller sources materials directly from trusted suppliers, eliminating factory markups and middleman fees. This streamlined approach guarantees competitive pricing, consistent quality, and real-time cost insights, every time.

Decentralized Manufacturing

Built Smarter, Delivered Faster

Building homes off-site in climate-controlled facilities eliminates the unpredictability of on-site construction. Fuller’s partnership with leading production facilities nationwide allows us to scale efficiently while maintaining standardized processes and consistent quality. By supplying materials directly, we ensure uniformity in every build, no matter the location.
